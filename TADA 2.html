Manchester City is reportedly fast-tracking negotiations with Bayer Leverkusen’s midfield prodigy Florian Wirtz. Rodri Makes History with 2024 Ballon d’Or

Rodri made history by winning the 2024 Ballon d’Or, becoming the first Spanish male player since 1960 to claim the award. After leading Manchester City to Premier League success and shining at Euro 2024, his win stirred debate, with Cristiano Ronaldo suggesting Vinícius Jr. deserved it more.

Oscar Bobb – City’s Next Big Thing?

Oscar Bobb, 22, has emerged as a promising winger for Manchester City. After breaking into the first team in 2023 with key goals against Newcastle and in the Champions League, injuries—including a leg fracture in 2024—slowed his progress. Now back, he’s earning praise from Pep Guardiola and showing flashes of his creative talent. Known for his quick feet, vision, and composure, Bobb is pushing to secure a regular spot and become one of City’s next big attacking threats.
