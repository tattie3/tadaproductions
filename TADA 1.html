Phil Foden – From Boyhood Dream to Global Star

Born in Stockport, Phil Foden joined Manchester City's academy at nine and rose to stardom with dazzling skill and vision. After leading England’s U-17s to World Cup glory in 2017, he became a key figure in City’s golden era, winning multiple Premier League titles, domestic cups, and a historic treble. Today, Foden is one of England’s brightest talents, shining for both club and country.

Erling Haaland – The Goal Machine

Born in Leeds, England, and raised in Norway, Erling Haaland quickly became one of football’s most feared strikers. After shining at RB Salzburg and Borussia Dortmund, he joined Manchester City in 2022, breaking scoring records and helping the club win a historic treble. Known for his incredible speed, power, and finishing, Haaland is already one of the brightest stars in world football.
